## 4. AI automation course for non-technical professionals 2026

For a non‑technical professional who wants **AI automation skills by 2026**, look for short, practical programs that focus on AI tools, workflows, and no‑code automation rather than coding or data science.[1][2]

Below are some of the most relevant options and how they differ.

### 1. TripleTen – **AI Automation Bootcamp** (strong fit for “AI automation”)

- **Profile:** Career changers and professionals who want to build automation solutions, not just use chatbots.[2]  
- **Duration / Format:** ~4 months, part‑time, online with interactive lessons and projects.[2]  
- **Focus:**  
  - AI tools and prompt engineering  
  - **Automation workflows**, AI‑powered apps  
  - **APIs and RPA (robotic process automation)**  
  - AI agents and technical integrations  
  - Capstone project for your portfolio[2]  
- **Tech requirement:** Designed so **no prior tech background is required**; support and tutoring are emphasized.[2]  
- **Best if you want:** A structured path into **AI automation specialist / operations / workflow** roles.

### 2. Short, non‑technical “AI at work” courses (good if you want automation plus productivity)

From the roundup of **15 AI bootcamps in 2026**, several options focus on practical AI and light/no coding for professionals.[2]

**a) Institute of Data – Practical AI Short Course**

- **Profile:** Professionals who want to **use AI tools effectively at work without learning programming**.[2]  
- **Duration / Format:** 10 weeks part‑time or 1 week full‑time, live online with labs and projects.[2]  
- **Focus:**  
  - Generative AI basics  
  - Prompting  
  - AI‑assisted productivity, research, analysis, creativity  
  - **Workflow design with AI tools**[2]  
- **Best if you want:** Broad, practical AI literacy and to start building semi‑automated workflows inside tools you already use (Docs, email, CRM, etc.).

**b) 4Geeks Academy – AI for Professionals** *(mentioned in the same article)*

- **Profile:** Professionals who want **hands‑on AI skills** with mentorship and no technical background.[2]  
- **Duration / Format:** ~6‑week, live, mentor‑supported online format.[2]  
- **Focus:**  
  - Prompting  
  - Text and image generation  
  - **No‑code automation and AI workflows**  
  - Workplace productivity with AI tools[2]  
- **Best if you want:** A short, guided program that gets you from basic AI to building simple automated workflows without coding.

### 3. Very short intros for absolute beginners

If you want to “test the waters” before a bootcamp:

**Dataquest – AI Chatbots (free mini‑course)**

- **Duration:** ~3 hours, self‑paced, online.[2]  
- **Focus:**  
  - Basics of chatbots and LLMs  
  - Prompt writing and interacting effectively with AI assistants[2]  
- **Best if you want:** **AI literacy** and better prompting so you can later build more advanced automation.[2]
